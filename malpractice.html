Medical Malpractice

Whether it be the negligence of a doctor, dentist, or pharmacist, we can help with various medical malpractice claims.

What is Professional Negligence/Medical Malpractice?

Sometimes professionals such as doctors or pharmacists make mistakes in their professional duties that result in injuries, pain and suffering. When professionals do not perform their duties at a certain level of care, and you are injured, you can speak to an experienced attorney to discuss the strength of you case. Attorneys are able to navigate the complicated procedural legal rules and regulations in these types of cases.

Common types of Professional Negligence and Medical Malpractice include:

  • Misdiagnosis
  • Delayed Diagnosis
  • Failing to Notice Drug Interactions
  • Prescribing the Wrong Medication
  • Given the Wrong Dosage

What do I need to bring when I meet with an attorney to evaluate my case?

Lawsuits can be a lengthy process, and its best to keep any documentation, information related to your case. The attorney will ask questions about what happened. As time passes, your memory may fade. While the incident is fresh in your memory, write down any details that may be relevant and collect any documents, receipts, bills, or anything that may be related to your potential case. The attorney handling your case will follow up with you to gather information about the case and demographic information.
